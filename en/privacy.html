Privacy Policy

App: Guchi Hole / Last updated: June 15, 2026

This policy describes how Guchi Hole handles data.

1. Core Privacy Principle

Guchi Hole is designed so that the complaint or feeling text you type is not saved and is not sent to the server.

2. Data We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect the text you type into the disposal field.
  • We do not store your typed complaint text on our server.
  • We do not require login.

3. Data Stored on Your Device

The app stores the following locally on your device using iOS local storage: your total disposal count, title, premium purchase status, selected skin, sound settings, onboarding status, login stamp progress, and observation stamp progress. Deleting the app may delete this local data.

4. Data Stored on the Server

The server stores only shared app statistics: today’s disposal count, total black hole mass, and the date key used for daily reset. These values are stored using Cloudflare Workers and Durable Objects.

5. Purchase Information

Premium purchases are processed by Apple through StoreKit and the App Store. The app checks purchase entitlements to unlock premium features. We do not receive your full payment card details.

6. Analytics and Logs

Cloudflare may provide operational metrics such as request counts, error counts, and performance data for the Worker. These are used to maintain service reliability and do not include the text you type into the app.

7. Children

The app is not intended to knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact the developer.

8. International Processing

Server infrastructure may process operational data in regions where Cloudflare operates. The text you type is not sent to the server.

9. Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy as the app evolves. The updated version will be posted on this page.

10. Contact

For privacy questions, contact us at kokicode.dev@gmail.com.
